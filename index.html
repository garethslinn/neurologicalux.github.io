About the Book

Empower Every User. Design Beyond Limits.

In today's digital era, creating accessible designs isn't just a best practice, it's a necessity. "Neurological UX" is your comprehensive guide to understanding and embracing the nuances of neurodiversity in user experience design.

Discover how to craft digital interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also genuinely inclusive for users with neurological differences such as autism, ADHD, dyslexia, OCD, and anxiety. This book moves beyond traditional accessibility guidelines, offering insights and strategies tailored specifically for designers and UX professionals.

Inside, you'll explore

  • Deep Dives into Neurodiversity: Understand how various neurological dispositions affect user interaction and perception.
  • Beyond the Basics: Expand your knowledge past color contrasts and scalable fonts to include cognitive accessibility considerations.
  • Practical Design Strategies: Learn from real-world examples and apply evidence-based techniques to your projects.
  • Preparing for WCAG 3.0: Get ahead with insights aligned with upcoming accessibility standards focusing on neurological aspects.
  • Flexible Frameworks: Implement adaptable best practices that cater to a wide spectrum of user needs without compromising on creativity.
